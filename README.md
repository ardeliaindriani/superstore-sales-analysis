# Sales Performance Analysis — Retail Superstore

## Overview
SQL-based analysis of retail sales data to uncover revenue patterns, 
profitability drivers, and discount impact across regions, segments, and products.

## Business Questions
1. Which product category generates the highest revenue?
2. Which region is most profitable?
3. Which customer segment orders most frequently?
4. What is the monthly sales trend?
5. Does higher discount always lead to higher profit?
6. What are the top 10 products by sales?
7. Which customer segment has the most loss-making orders?

## Key Findings
- Technology leads revenue at 36.4% of total sales
- West region is most profitable (108K total profit)
- High discounts (>40%) result in average loss of -106 per order
- Consumer segment has highest volume but lowest profit efficiency

## Tools
- SQLite (via SQLiteOnline.com)
- Dataset: [Superstore Sales — Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)
